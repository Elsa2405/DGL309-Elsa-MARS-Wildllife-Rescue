Welcome to MARS Wildlife rescue

CARE FOR ANIMALS | MARS WILDLIFE RESCUE

At MARS Wildlife Rescue, we are dedicated to the rescue, rehabilitation, and release of injured and orphaned wildlife. Our mission is to protect local ecosystems and educate the community about wildlife conservation. Join us in making a difference!

  • 32 years

    Since our founding, we have grown into a trusted resource for wildlife care in the region.

  • 600 animals

    We have successfully rescued and rehabilitated thousands of animals, showcasing our commitment to wildlife conservation.

  • 50 team members

    Our passionate team includes experts in wildlife care, dedicated to making a difference in the lives of animals.

  • 500 workshops

    We conduct numerous educational workshops each year, fostering community engagement and awareness about wildlife conservation.
